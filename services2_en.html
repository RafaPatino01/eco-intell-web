Our Services

TECHNICAL SERVICES

Our Technical Services offer specialized solutions for environmental management, from the design of treatment systems to impact assessment and the development of sustainable projects.

Waste Treatment Design

We create engineering solutions for waste and wastewater treatment in agricultural, livestock, and industrial sectors.
Environmental Management Systems

We develop systems based on ISO 14001 and ISO 50001 standards for efficient resource and energy management.
Environmental Impact Studies

We assess the potential impact of projects on the environment, identifying mitigation and sustainability measures.
Environmental Risk Studies and Analysis

We identify and evaluate environmental risks to develop prevention and management strategies.
Assessment and Remediation of Contaminated Soils

We diagnose and treat contaminated soils, restoring soil quality and preventing damage.
Environmental and Energy Audits

We assess the environmental and energy performance of your company, identifying improvement opportunities.
Emissions Inventory and Water Footprint

We measure greenhouse gas emissions and water use to enhance efficiency.
Hydrogeological Studies

We analyze the characteristics and behavior of aquifers for sustainable groundwater management.
Subproduct and Waste Management Plans

We design strategies to reduce, reuse, and recycle subproducts and waste, following the circular economy principles.
Bioenergy Projects Development

We create sustainable energy solutions using biogas and other bioenergy sources.
Economic Evaluation of Environmental Projects

We analyze the costs, benefits, and viability of environmental projects for informed decision-making.
Environmental Training Programs

We offer training programs to raise environmental awareness and improve sustainable practices.
